What do we consider a "bad law"?

We consider anything that is against the laws of Human Rights to be a bad law.

What do you consider "Human Rights"?

Laws that prevent humanistic freedom, i.e anti-GSRM/anti-LGBTQ+ laws and laws that are against religious freedom.

Terminology

  • GSRM - Gender, Sexuality and Romantic Minorities. An inclusive term for LGBTQ+
  • LGBTQ+ - Lesbian, Gay, Bi, Trans, Queer and more minorities
